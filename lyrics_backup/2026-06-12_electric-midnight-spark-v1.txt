[RAW LYRICS]
[Hook]
Midnight shadows dancing on your skin
I can feel the fire about to begin
A single look and I am pulling close
You are the only truth I want to know

[Verse 1]
The steady hum of the empty road
Leaving behind the heavy load
A fading amber in the coffee cup
When I reach out, you are looking up

[Pre-Chorus]
Your breath against my collar now
We are losing track of time somehow
A quiet rhythm in the fading light
I want to keep you through the endless night

[Chorus]
You are my electric midnight spark
Lighting up the quiet in the dark
Every touch is like a burning flame
I just want to hear you call my name

[Post-Chorus]
[Echo] (Call my name, oh call my name)
Hold me close and stoke the flame

[Verse 2]
A gentle breeze through the open door
Leaving us wanting a little more
I trace the lines across your hand
We are drifting away from the lonely land

[Echo] (Call my name, oh call my name)

[Bridge]
Whispers wrapping round the empty space
Fading out the world to find this place
I am lost within the rhythm we create
A beautiful collision, sealing fate
Even Saeed could never break this spell
Falling deeper than I care to tell

[Spoken]
[Whispered] (I never want this night to end)
[Whispered] (Just keep looking at me like that)
